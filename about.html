ABOUT pop!

At Pop! we want you to have fun with your food.

We carry a variety of flavorful, fruity teas and smoothies and you can customize your drink with traditional boba or (our favorite) popping boba.

Traditional boba is chewy and has a texture similar to a gummy bear.

Popping boba is made of a thin gel-like coating filled with fruit-flavored juice. As the name implies, it pops in your mouth. Give it a try!
